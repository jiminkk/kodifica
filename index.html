About

We at Kodifica have traveled far and wide to search for the best kbbq restaurants around the world. From the concrete jungle of Los Angeles to the tech-craved, disruptive haven of San Francisco, our team has committed countless hours into researching what makes a Kogi restaurant, well, a Kogi restaurant. The purpose of our research is to make Kodifica a Kogi experience unlike any other, by combining the best attributes and features of Kogi repos around the world.
